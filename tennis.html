Bridge & Cards

Bridge & Cards: A grandiose sophistication marks the air conditioned card and bridge rooms. This sport was aparticular
favourite of Lt. HH Yeshwant Rao Holkar, himself an excellent player. Every year the club hosts the
‘Maharaja Yeshwant Rao Holkar National Bridge Championship’ in fond memory of The Maharaja. The activity
attracts over thirty players every day with a calender of a regular weekly tournament.
