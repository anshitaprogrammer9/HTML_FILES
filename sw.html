SWIMMING POOL

Swimming Pool & Health Complex: The Club has two pools for club members and their dependents. One is exclusively for ladies and the new half olympic size
pool is for competitive sports, where competitions in various events are also held. Separate health centres for men and
women with Jacuzzi, steam and sauna complete the experience. The facilities extend to Yoga, Aerobics and acupressure
therapy rooms.
