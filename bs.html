Billiards And Snooker

Billiards And Snooker: An air conditioned Billiards room with two
tables of international standard, is a regular venue for National and Prize-money championships. Asian Billiards and
under 21 Snooker championships have been hosted under the auspices of the club, with an impressive participation of
close to 20 Asian countries. Renowned Billiard and Snooker players from Michael Ferriera , Geet Sethi to Ashok Shandilya,
have all cued the ball at this venue. The Ex-National Ladies Champion Ms Pooja Bharkatia groomed her skills in the Club
itself. Club can boast of producing many state champions, naming few Bobji Nayudu, Sunil Bajaj, Ajay Agrawal, Ritesh Lunkad,
Kanishk Jhanjaria, Ketan Chawla etc.
